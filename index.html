/ 01 — About

Video that earns
its runtime.

I am a video editor and content strategist based in Lagos, Nigeria. My background is technical — B.Eng in Chemical Engineering — which means I think in systems, not just aesthetics. Every cut has a reason. Every frame earns its place.

I work across short-form and long-form — YouTube deep-dives, TikTok hooks, brand explainers, and B2B product content. At Biofil Technologies I built their full video content workflow from scratch: from diagnosing why their uploads looked degraded, to fixing the compression pipeline, to writing the framework their team uses today.

I use AI tools actively and deliberately — not to replace craft, but to do in two hours what used to take six. Runway for generative B-roll, CapCut AI for speed edits, HeyGen for video avatars, and GitHub Copilot when the project crosses into code.

I also run my own YouTube channel as a live experiment — testing hooks, A/B-ing thumbnails, reading retention graphs and iterating. The 1,500+ views on a single upload came from doing that work properly.

/ 07 — Creative Philosophy

Design isn't just about "looking good" — it's about visual communication. Whether it's a book cover that must tell a story in three seconds or a brand identity that needs to feel trustworthy, I focus on the psychology of the viewer.

My background in engineering gives me a structured approach to design systems. I don't just pick colors; I build palettes that evoke specific emotions. I don't just place text; I establish clear visual hierarchies that guide the eye to the most important message.
